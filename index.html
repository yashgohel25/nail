Our Story

Welcome to Janki Nail Art

Hello everyone!! My name is Pooja Gohil, I am a certified Nail Technician.

I started my journey on 29 August 2022 and participated in the Surat Ka Khubsurat Expo in Surat, India. With my constant hard work and dedication, I won the fifth prize at that expo. After that experience, there was no going back!!

To take my career one step ahead, I participated in Aakar Expo and Beasa International. I was recognized in the top 20 World Book of Records London.

I am immensely passionate towards nails and love to show my creativity through nails.
